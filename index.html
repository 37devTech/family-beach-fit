About

Family Beach Fit is more than a bootcamp—it's a community built around transformation. Founded by California native Kevin Brown, a former Men’s Physique competitor and devoted family man, this bootcamp helps people grow stronger not just physically, but mentally, spiritually, and as a family. Kevin believes fitness should be empowering, not toxic. His Christian faith and relationship with God are the foundation of his life, his family, and his approach to wellness. He aims to shift the focus away from social media comparison and toward meaningful health for your family, career, and personal well-being.

Meet the Trainer

Kevin Brown

Certified Personal Trainer & Former Men’s Physique Competitor

With a faith-centered mindset and a heart for community, Kevin leads every session with encouragement, integrity, and purpose. Join him on the beach and take your first step toward total transformation.
