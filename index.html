About

Family Beach Fit is more than a bootcamp—it's a community built around transformation. Founded by California native Kevin Brown, a former Men’s Physique competitor and dedicated family man, this bootcamp helps people grow stronger not just physically, but mentally and spiritually. Kevin believes fitness should be empowering, not toxic. He aims to shift focus away from social media comparison and toward meaningful health for your family, career, and personal well-being.

Services

  • Full-Body Bootcamp Circuits: High-intensity circuits using battle ropes, kettlebells, dumbbells, resistance bands, and bodyweight exercises.
  • Battle Rope Focus Sessions: 30–45 minute sessions with power slams, waves, and rope drills to build endurance and strength.
  • Beginner to Advanced Ropes Coaching: Learn proper form, progress to combos, and avoid injury.
  • Partner & Group Challenges: Fun team-based games like relays and timed rope workouts.
  • HIIT + Battle Rope Fusion: Combine ropes with sprints, plyometrics, and core workouts for fat-burning.
  • Kids & Teens Bootcamp: Fun, age-appropriate sessions focusing on coordination, teamwork, and fitness basics.
  • Beach Circuit Bootcamps: Outdoor workouts using sand resistance and ocean views for a full-body challenge.
  • One-on-One Personal Training: Personalized rope and strength-based training for all fitness goals.
  • Corporate Wellness Circuits: Group sessions to build endurance, communication, and team morale.
  • Fitness Challenges & Events: Monthly bootcamps, obstacle races, and rewards to keep you motivated.
